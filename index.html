UPCOMING TOURS & DESTINATION

After monsoon in India is called post-monsoon or retreating monsoon, which lasts from October to November. This is a great time to travel to many parts of India, as the weather is pleasant and sunny. The monsoon rains have subsided, leaving the landscapes lush and green. Temperatures are moderate, ranging between 25 and 34 °C (77 and 93 °F) in the southern parts of India.
